Welcome to London! London is the capital city of England and the United Kingdom. With a population of over 9 million people, it is one of the most vibrant and diverse cities in the world. London is known for its rich history, dating back to Roman times when it was called Londinium.

Over the centuries, it has grown into a global center of finance, culture, education, and politics. The city is home to iconic landmarks such as the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, the London Eye, and St Paul's Cathedral.

Visitors can explore world-renowned museums like the British Museum, the National Gallery, and the Victoria and Albert Museum, many of which offer free admission.
