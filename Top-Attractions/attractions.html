London Eye

The London Eye is a giant Ferris wheel on the South Bank of the River Thames in London. It is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the UK.

Tower of London

The Tower of London is a historic castle located on the north bank of the River Thames in central London. It has served as a royal palace, fortress, and prison.

Buckingham Palace

Buckingham Palace is the London residence and administrative headquarters of the monarch of the United Kingdom.

Big Ben

Big Ben is the nickname for the Great Bell of the clock at the north end of the Palace of Westminster in London. It is one of the most iconic landmarks in the city.

British Museum

The British Museum is a public museum dedicated to human history, art, and culture. It houses a vast collection of world art and artifacts.
